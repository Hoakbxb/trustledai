Advisory Services

What we help you with

Every engagement is built around the TrustLed AI Readiness Framework, a structured methodology grounded in zero-trust cybersecurity principles that ensures your organisation has answered the right questions before AI goes live.

●

AI Readiness Assessment

A structured assessment using our six-domain framework: AI system inventory, authority and permissions, risk assessment, monitoring plan, accountability, and lifecycle management. You get a written report showing exactly where your organisation stands and what needs to be in place before AI is deployed.

●

ISO 42001 Support

Gap analysis against ISO 42001, the AI management systems standard. Implementation support to get you from where you are to certification readiness. Whether you need a gap analysis to understand the distance or full implementation support, we work at your pace.

●

AI Policy & Governance Design

AI acceptable use policies, authority boundary documents, accountability structures, incident response plans, and monitoring frameworks. Practical governance documentation your team can implement and follow, designed around how people actually work.

●

AI Risk Assessment

Comprehensive risk assessments for your AI systems using our six-dimension scoring model. Risk tier classification, vendor risk evaluation, and third-party AI risk analysis. The output is always something your team can act on and your board can understand.

How It Works

From conversation to implementation

01

Initial Conversation

A 30-minute call to understand your situation: where AI fits in your business, what you have in place, and what you need. No charge, no obligation.

02

Scoping and Proposal

We scope the engagement based on what you actually need, not a one-size-fits-all package. You get a clear proposal with deliverables, timeline, and pricing.

03

Assessment and Analysis

We work alongside your team to assess your AI systems, policies, risk exposure, and governance posture against the readiness framework and relevant standards.

04

Deliverables and Implementation

You receive actionable documentation: readiness reports, policies, risk registers, governance frameworks, and a clear roadmap for what to do next. We can support implementation or hand over to your team.

Agentic AI Services

AI agent deployment with governance built in

For organisations that have completed the readiness assessment and are ready to deploy AI agents, we offer end-to-end agentic AI services. We scope the governance requirements, manage the technical deployment, and ensure every agent operates within defined authority boundaries with monitoring and accountability from day one.

●

Agent Design & Configuration

Architecture design, platform selection, tool integrations, authority boundaries, escalation thresholds, and monitoring triggers. Every technical decision documented.

●

Testing & Deployment

Pre-deployment testing against realistic scenarios, edge cases, and boundary conditions. Deployment with full governance documentation: system register entry, authority boundary document, accountability matrix.

●

Ongoing Management

Optional monthly retainer for monitoring, performance reviews, updates, incident response, and reporting. You focus on your business. We keep the agent running properly and governed.
