Who this is for

People who want a career in AI GRC

You are a career beginner looking for your first role in AI governance, risk, and compliance. Or you are working in another field entirely and you want structured, job-facing training to move into AI GRC or AI auditing. Or you are a young professional who wants practical AI governance skills you can monetise. Whatever your starting point, this programme takes you from where you are to the job market. No prior experience required.

SME leaders who need their teams AI-ready

You are running a business or leading a team, and AI is either already in your operations or about to be. You need your people to understand what is at stake, what should be in place before AI is deployed, and how to avoid the mistakes that are costing other organisations money, reputation, and regulatory trouble. This workshop is delivered to your team, online or in person.

Frequently Asked Questions

I have no background in AI, compliance, or tech. Can I join?
Yes. The programme starts from zero. No prior knowledge required. If you can think critically and communicate clearly, you have the foundation. We teach you the rest.
Will this actually help me get a job?
The programme is built around skills employers hire for. You leave with a portfolio piece, a career action plan, and alumni community access with job leads. We cannot guarantee a job, but you will be more prepared than most candidates.
Can I freelance after the bootcamp?
Yes. Both tracks include a module on freelancing. Track A: AI governance advisory. Track B: independent AI auditing.
Can my employer pay?
Yes. We provide a formal invoice and a justification letter for your employer's L&D budget.
Will I get a certificate?
Yes. All completers receive a TrustLed AI Academy certificate specifying their track.
Is this accredited for CPE credits?
We are pursuing CPE credit approval from ISACA and IAPP. We will update participants when confirmed.
What if I miss a live session?
Recordings available for 48 hours. You may miss up to 3 of 16 sessions. Workshop: full attendance expected.
What is the cancellation policy?
Full refund up to 14 days before. After that, transfer to the next cohort subject to availability.
How do I register?
Email hello@trustledai.com with your name, the programme and track you want. We will send registration and payment details.
